Bake Buddy

My first little web app I made for my wife to solve her frustration converting baking ingredients from US imperial to grams. Bake Buddy uses simple Javascript to carry out conversion calculations for common baking ingredients. Ingredients are converted from a volume(US cups) to a weight(grams).
