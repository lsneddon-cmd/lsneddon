About me

As long as I can remember, I have always had my head buried in non-fiction books prying out new and interesting knowledge. Learning is something that has always excited me and it will never stop.

For me, coding is unlike any other subject as I can take what I have learned and make it my own; all the while expanding my knowledge base and, hence, my expressive capacity for my creativity.

There are a multitude of things that I enjoy learning; though where coding is different is in the freedom of application of knowledge. When I think up, design, implement and deploy new projects; I experience a real feeling of freedom of expression. Every part of the lifecycle is a true pleasure for me, as is learning new languages, tools and frameworks.
